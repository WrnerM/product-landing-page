Design

Features

The Nissan Z, an iconic nameplate in the world of sports cars, is celebrated for its blend of performance, design, and modern technology. The latest iteration of the Z features a powerful 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine, delivering an exhilarating 400 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with either a 6-speed manual transmission for purists or a 9-speed automatic for those seeking effortless precision. The Z's rear-wheel-drive platform ensures a classic sports car feel, enhanced by a lightweight chassis and finely tuned suspension for exceptional handling. Advanced performance features such as a mechanical limited-slip differential, launch control, and adaptive damping further elevate the driving experience, making it equally at home on winding roads or the track.

Performance

The Nissan Z delivers thrilling performance with its 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine, generating 400 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque for exhilarating acceleration. Drivers can choose between a 6-speed manual transmission with a high-performance clutch for an engaging experience or a 9-speed automatic with paddle shifters for seamless precision. Built on a rear-wheel-drive platform, the Z offers a classic sports car feel, enhanced by a lightweight chassis and a meticulously tuned suspension system. Performance-focused features like a mechanical limited-slip differential, launch control, and available adaptive dampers ensure exceptional handling and stability, making the Nissan Z equally adept on winding roads, highway stretches, and track days.
